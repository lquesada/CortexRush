Cortex Rush Privacy Policy

This policy outlines how Cortex Rush handles your data. Our goal is transparency and simplicity.

Key Principle: Cortex Rush does not submit any of your personal data to our servers. All game data and processing happen locally on your device.

Data Collection

Cortex Rush does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require you to create an account, register, or provide an email address.

Game Progress and Statistics

All your game progress, statistics, and settings are stored locally on your device.

  • Local Storage: Your data remains entirely under your control.
  • Cloud Backup: Cortex Rush may utilize standard Android auto-backup features if enabled on your device, securely backing up your data to your personal Google Drive account. We do not have access to this data.
  • Manual Export: You can choose to manually export your game data as a backup file. This file is saved securely on your device.

Permissions

Cortex Rush only requests permissions strictly necessary for its core functionality:

  • Storage (optional on newer Android versions): Used solely if you explicitly choose to export or import a manual backup of your game data.
  • Notifications: The app may schedule local notifications (e.g., daily reminders to play). These are entirely generated on your device and are not pushed from any external server.

Third-Party Services and Ads

Cortex Rush does not include any third-party analytics trackers, advertising networks, or data-sharing SDKs. The app is completely ad-free and respects your privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy in the future, we will provide notice by updating the effective date at the bottom of this page. Since we do not collect contact information, we encourage you to review this page periodically.
