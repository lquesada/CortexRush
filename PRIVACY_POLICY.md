# Privacy Policy for Cortex Rush

The **Cortex Rush** app (the "App") is provided by **Luis Quesada Torres** ("the Author"). This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is to assist users with cognitive and brain training exercises.

### 1. Complete Privacy & No Data Collection

The App is designed from the ground up to respect user privacy.

* **Personal Information:** The Author does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or physical location).
* **Device & Usage Data:** The App does not log device identifiers, IP addresses, or any technical telemetry regarding your brain training sessions.
* **100% Offline Functionality:** Cortex Rush operates entirely locally on your device. It does not require an internet connection to perform its core training functions, ensuring your data stays yours.

### 2. Zero Third-Party Tracking

The App does **not** integrate with any third-party service providers. There are no advertising networks, analytics platforms, or external crash-reporting SDKs embedded in the app that could track your activity or harvest your data.

### 3. Device Permissions

Any permissions requested by Cortex Rush on your Android device are utilized strictly to facilitate the local functionality and responsiveness of the brain training games. No data accessed through these permissions is ever stored off-device, uploaded, or shared with anyone.

### 4. Protection of Children’s Privacy

Cortex Rush is safe for users of all ages. It does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Because the App features zero data collection across the board, it inherently complies with global privacy standards regarding minors.

### 5. Policy Updates

The Author may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any modifications will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document and pushing the updated text directly to the official GitHub repository.

### 6. Contact & Support

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this privacy policy, please open an **Issue** on the official GitHub repository link provided above.
